About Us

Therapy Rooted in Compassion & Expertise

Hability Therapy was founded with one mission: to empower every individual to reach their full potential through personalized, evidence-based occupational therapy.

Our team brings deep experience across pediatric and adult OT, sensory processing, motor development, and daily living skills. We believe in treating the whole person — not just the diagnosis.

We serve families across Middlesex, Monmouth, and Ocean counties, with in-home visits, clinic sessions, and telehealth options to fit every family's needs.
