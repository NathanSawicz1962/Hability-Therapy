Hability Therapy

Services Provided

Hability Therapy provides habilitative occupational therapy services that support individuals in developing, maintaining, and improving essential life skills needed for daily living and meaningful participation.

Our services focus on functional, real-world skills across home, community, and day program settings, including:

Activities of Daily Living (ADLs) & Functional Skills: Dressing, grooming, hygiene, and self-care routines; meal participation and feeding support; and building independence and consistency in daily routines.

Motor Planning, Coordination & Functional Strength: Improving motor planning and sequencing for daily tasks; fine motor skill development and bilateral coordination; and functional strengthening to support participation in everyday activities.

Sensory Processing & Regulation: Addressing sensory processing differences that impact daily functioning; developing individualized sensory regulation strategies; and supporting attention, transitions, and emotional regulation.

Wheelchair Evaluations & Assistive Technology: Functional wheelchair and seating evaluations; collaboration with wheelchair clinics and ATP providers; and assistive technology to support independence and safety.

Community & Day Program Participation: Supporting engagement in structured and unstructured activities; improving participation in day programs and community routines; and promoting carryover of skills across environments.

Executive Functioning & Life Skills: Task initiation, organization, and follow-through; building independence with routines and responsibilities; and supporting functional problem-solving skills.
