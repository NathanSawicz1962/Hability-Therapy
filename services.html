Services Provided

Hability Therapy provides habilitative occupational therapy services that support individuals in developing, maintaining, and improving essential life skills needed for daily living and meaningful participation.

Activities of Daily Living (ADLs) & Functional Skills: Dressing, grooming, hygiene, meal participation, and building independence.

Motor Planning, Coordination & Functional Strength: Fine motor skill development, sequencing, and functional strengthening for daily activities.

Sensory Processing & Regulation: Developing individualized sensory strategies and supporting attention and emotional regulation.

Wheelchair Evaluations & Assistive Technology: Functional evaluations and assistive technology to support independence and safety.

Community & Day Program Participation: Engagement in structured/unstructured activities and carryover of skills across environments.

Executive Functioning & Life Skills: Task initiation, organization, problem-solving, and functional independence.
