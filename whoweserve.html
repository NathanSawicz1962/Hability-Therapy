Who We Serve

Hability Therapy supports adolescents and adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD) across home, community, and program settings. We partner with individuals, families, and support teams to build functional life skills, promote independence, and support meaningful participation in daily routines and community life.

As Hability Therapy continues to grow, we remain committed to expanding services to meet evolving needs and to support additional individuals and communities through thoughtful, inclusive, and person-centered occupational therapy services.

We are dedicated to removing the social and systemic barriers that often make it difficult for marginalized groups to find high-quality rehabilitative care. Our clinic is a space where diversity is the standard, not the exception, and where people of all gender identities and racial backgrounds can find practitioners who truly listen to them. We believe that by creating an environment free of judgment and bias, we allow our clients to focus entirely on their growth and functional independence.
