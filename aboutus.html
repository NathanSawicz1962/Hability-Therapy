About Hability Therapy

Meet Nirel Meadows, OTR/L

Nirel Meadows is a licensed Occupational Therapist with over 15 years of experience in the intellectual and developmental disabilities field, supporting individuals across the lifespan in building meaningful, functional skills for everyday life. Her professional background within the DDD system provides a strong foundation for understanding the real-world needs, challenges, and supports that impact long-term independence.

Prior to founding Hability Therapy, LLC, Nirel held a variety of roles within the DDD service system, including Direct Support Professional (DSP), Group Home Manager, and Day Program Supervisor. These experiences shaped her practical, systems-informed approach to occupational therapy and continue to influence how services are designed, implemented, and carried over across settings.

Nirel specializes in habilitative occupational therapy for adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities, primarily through the DDD Supports Waiver. Her clinical focus includes activities of daily living (ADLs), community participation, mobility and positioning, sensory regulation, assistive technology, and adaptive strategies that support functional performance in home, day program, and community environments.

Hability Therapy emphasizes individualized, goal-driven services with a strong focus on collaboration among individuals, families, caregivers, support coordinators, and interdisciplinary teams. Nirel is committed to delivering therapy that is respectful, practical, and measurable—supporting individuals in building skills that promote safety, confidence, and meaningful participation in daily life.
