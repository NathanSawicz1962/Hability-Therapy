About Hability Therapy

Hability Therapy is a dedicated provider of occupational therapy services, committed to helping individuals of all ages build independence, confidence, and meaningful participation in everyday life.

Our mission is rooted in personalized, evidence-based care. We partner closely with clients and their families to create treatment plans that reflect real-world goals, functional needs, and personal strengths.

Our therapists take a holistic approach, addressing physical abilities, emotional well-being, and environmental factors to support long-term success beyond the therapy session.

At Hability Therapy, we believe progress happens best in a supportive, respectful environment where every individual feels heard, valued, and empowered.
